Invi
An Inventory Management System


| About |


Invi is a robust inventory management solution designed to meet the specific needs of businesses. The software efficiently tracks incoming and outgoing stock, ensuring real-time inventory updates and dynamically rendered bills including Real-Time Inventory Tracking, Supplier Profiles and Dynamic Billing.


| Features |


  • Interactive charts display current available stock.
  • Detailed stock information is maintained, including current available stock.
  • Incoming transactions are managed through purchases.
  • Outgoing transactions are managed through sales.
  • All transactions performed through the software are recorded.
  • Bills are dynamically generated and can be printed.
  • Individual profiles are maintained for each supplier.
